  • About Ξ [ TRINOLINE ] 
    • This page is about announcements for Minori's new title, 『TRINOLINE（トリノライン / TRIノLINE）』 .

      Although small, the island is ideally sized for its purpose as an experimental location for androids. The distance from the mainland is also suitable for that purpose.

      In an environment with a distinct “pacific island“ atmosphere, a boy and his parents encounter their “family“ who were left behind in the sorrow of the past. While reluctant, the parents too were happy to return to that place.

      Now that the boy has grown up and is once again living with his family, surrounded by the nice memories of the past; the loss of his younger sister in her youthful, innocent years does not seem so bad.


      An android girl with memories of a lost family. A boy who yearns for the lost time with his sister. The two share their life together with the adults around them. It could have lead them to “ happiness “ .

      At least for the inventor of the android.
      And at least for the people who wish for their future.


      On an island where humans and androids coexist, the two first find “hope”; then the fickleness of “reality”; and perhaps finally, the meaning of “life” itself.

      No matter one's attempt to stop, or to return to a certain point in life, the inexorable passing of time permits but a single direction. Forward. Their youth too will lead in that direction.


      In those moments.
      Even then, I...


      ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯ ¯

       “Living” in a life where time flows.
      Living in a time where people go forward at their own youthful pace.
      And for those people who go forward, the possibility of seeing a “faint light” in front of them.

      This title, 『Trinoline』,
      is about the people who follow that faint light that leads them towards their goal.


      All the power of expression created up till now has attempted to convey the inherent energy in this R-18 title.
      And to express the radiant beauty of 'budding romantic relationships' that were first depicted in
      『Soreyori no Prologue(ソレヨリノ前奏詩)』・『Tsumi no Hikari Rendezvous(罪ノ光ランデヴー)』.

      In addition, the narrative of this “ R-18 PC game ” also examines, 「Whom you want to seek the meaning of life with and what you want to seek in your live」. Please look forward to it.


      2016.07.20. minori Staff
