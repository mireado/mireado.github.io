  • Ξ Sara
    • >Tsumugi Sara ｜ 紬木 沙羅
      Sara(沙羅) was genius since she was young and was also a friend of Shun(舜) in her childhood.
      She is now working as a scientist who is involved in developing androids.

      Although she appears harsh and cold, that is only because she likes experiments,
      in actuality she has a kind and affectionate personality.

      She is the same age as Shun(舜) but has a greater level of maturity.
      Sometimes she plays around with Shun.

    • >Nanami Shun ｜ 七波 舜

      accepting other's opinions as much as possible.
